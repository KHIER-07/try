Japan

About Japan

Japan is an island country located in East Asia. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful landscapes, and advanced technology. The capital city is Tokyo, which is one of the most populous cities in the world.

Japan is famous for its traditional arts, including tea ceremonies, calligraphy, and flower arranging (ikebana). It is also renowned for its cuisine, such as sushi, ramen, and tempura.

The country is home to numerous UNESCO World Heritage Sites, stunning natural scenery, and a unique blend of ancient traditions and modern innovations.
